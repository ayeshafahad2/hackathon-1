## вңЁ Animations You'll Notice

### On Page Load
1. **Badge** fades in from top (0.8s)
2. **Title** fades in from bottom (0.8s, delay 0.1s)
3. **Subtitle** fades in from bottom (0.8s, delay 0.2s)
4. **Buttons** fade in from bottom (0.8s, delay 0.3s)
5. **Stats** fade in from bottom (0.8s, delay 0.4s)
6. **Feature cards** stagger in (0.1s, 0.2s, 0.3s delays)

### Continuous Animations
1. **Background gradient** slowly shifts colors (25s cycle)
2. **Emoji icons** float up and down (3s cycle)
3. **Badge icon** bounces (2s cycle)
4. **Blob orbs** move around (8-10s cycle)

### On Hover
1. **Feature cards**: Lift up 12px + scale 1.02 + glow
2. **Buttons**: Lift up 2px + shadow increase
3. **Icons**: Scale 1.15 + rotate 10deg
4. **Links**: Underline animates from center
5. **Navbar links**: Bottom border grows
