### Question: "What is ZMP?"

**Response:**
```
Bipedal locomotion is the ability to walk on two legs - a defining 
characteristic of humanoid robots and one of the most challenging 
problems in robotics.

• **Zero Moment Point (ZMP)**: Point where net moment of inertial and 
  gravitational forces is zero
• **Center of Mass (CoM)**: Average position of all mass, must be 
  controlled for balance
• **Capture Point**: Point where robot can step to stop

📚 Source: Robot Kinematics and Motion → Bipedal Locomotion
📖 See also: What are Humanoid Robots?
```
